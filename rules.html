In Interstellar Duel, two players compete to win the most cards by choosing the strongest attributes.

Game Setup

  • Each player gets a deck of cards with celestial bodies.
  • Cards are face-down.

Gameplay

  1. Players draw a card each round.
  2. The active player picks an attribute to compare.
  3. Higher value for the chosen attribute wins the round.
  4. If tied, cards are discarded.
  5. Players alternate turns each round.

Winning the Game

The player with the most cards when one deck runs out wins.

Attributes

  • Size: Higher is better.
  • Mass: Higher is better.
  • Temperature: Higher is better.
  • Distance: Lower is better.
